***** VERNON VIPERS *****

Vernon is a city in the state NewYork and I choose Vipers as a mascot for this town which represents braveness

  • Vipers are a large family of snakes; the scientific name is Viperidae.
  • The family Viperidae includes adders, pit vipers (like rattlesnakes, cottonmouths and copperheads), the Gaboon viper, green vipers and horned vipers.
  • Generally more venomous vipers are in tropical areas, particularly South America and Africa.
